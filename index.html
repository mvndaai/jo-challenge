#JoJorgensenVictimlessCrimeChallenge
Post yourself doing something legal for you that would be a victimless crime in another state

Why?

Jo Jorgensen is the only presidential candidate that supports decriminalizing victimless crimes.

This challenge is to help her to get 15% in the polls so she is allowed to debate and raise awareness to how victimless crimes are overutilized for discrimination.

How to participate

  1. Search for a weird law somewhere where you don't live
  2. Find a crime that shouldn't exist and hurts no one
  3. Take a picture or video of you performing that act
  4. Share everywhere using #JoJorgensenVictimlessCrimeChallenge
  5. Challenge someone else

Examples

  • California, Carmel § 8.44.020 - Permit required for to shoes heels in excess of 2 inches
  • Illinois, Beardstown § 4-4 - All farm animals shall be prohibited
  • Missouri, Chaffee § 205.010 - Unlawful for any person to harbor any dangerous dog
  • New Jersey § 24:10-57.17 - No person shall sell, offer for sale, or distribute to the ultimate consumer any milk or cream that is not pasteurized
  • Pennsylvania § 2163 - Illegal to own or sell quaker parrots. No exceptions they will euthanize
  • Utah § 41-6a-1112 - You cannot ride a bike while holding something in one hand
  • Virginia, Virginia Beach § 23-3 - Anyone over 12 years old "trick or treating" is guilty of a Class 4 misdemeanor
